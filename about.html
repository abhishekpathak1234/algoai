About.

Abhishek Pathak — Founder & CEO. The operator behind the automation.

I build systems because I know what it takes to run a business.

I didn't start in a tech lab selling software. I spent years in the trenches—first at Amazon, learning the ruthless efficiency of global supply chains, and later as a D2C founder, wrestling with the daily friction of customer acquisition, fulfillment, and team scaling.

As an operator and builder, I learned the hard way that a tool is only as good as the process it supports. If the underlying logic is flawed, slapping an AI wrapper on it just makes the chaos happen faster.

That's why AlgoBridge Ai exists. Not to sell you 'the future of tech,' but to systematically diagnose your operational bottlenecks and deploy targeted automation that actually moves the needle. I don't care about shiny objects; I care about ROI, bandwidth, and building resilient businesses.
